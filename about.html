about

I'm a recent grad from UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business with a triple major in Data Science, Business, and Economics (with a domain emphasis in Human Behavior and Psychology). I'm passionate about data strategy, and believe analytics should always be both accessible and actionable. At Cal, most of my time went to sustainability consulting and teaching computer science fundamentals to kids in underserved communities.

Most recently, I was in the Intelligent Solutions Group (Business Insights Team) within John Deere, where I had the opportunity to apply NLP methods to analyze the company's sentiment footprint. Since then, I've freelanced for a startup in Berkeley to build their brand marketing efforts and social media presence, and consulted for a major data server producer on closing gaps within their circular economy. Currently, I'm actively seeking a position as a data engineer.

In my free time, you'll likely find me making YouTube videos, trying to skateboard, or analyzing other people's Enneagram types.

I'm a Type 3, in case you were wondering.
